## design decisions

### stone number: 3.1.5

- follows extant pattern: 3.1.X.research.*
- 3.1.3 = internal.product.code
- 3.1.4 = internal.factory
- 3.1.5 = reflection.product (new category)

### three reflections (not one)

- **audience**: empathy dimension — who benefits
- **rootcause**: depth dimension — why needed
- **premortem**: caution dimension — what could fail

one stone would conflate concerns; three keeps them distinct and reviewable.
