yes.

**answer**: prior behaviors can remain as-is (they still work). new behaviors will use the dual-blueprint structure. optionally, we can provide a migration tool that splits an old 3.3.blueprint into 3.3.0.workloops and 3.3.1.codepaths.

**verdict**: ✅ self-answered

**verdict**: ✅ self-answered
