# reflection: audience

## who is the audience?

**primary:** mechanic — the one who drives through the behavior route and writes tests

- needs clear guidance on what to produce at repros stone
- needs connection between repros promises and verification checks
- benefits from self-review prompts that catch gaps

**secondary:** foreman — the one who reviews PRs

- needs visibility into what the mechanic promised vs delivered
- benefits from snapshots that show caller experience
- relies on verification artifacts to confirm completeness

**tertiary:** wisher — the one who requested the behavior

- cares that the behavior works as envisioned
- benefits indirectly from more thorough test coverage
- trusts that mechanics verify before they deliver

---

## why do they care?

**mechanic:**
- pain: "i completed the route but foreman found gaps — embarrassed and reworked"
- goal: "pass verification on first attempt with complete coverage"
- frustration: "i didn't know what was expected until review"

**foreman:**
- pain: "i keep to find absent journey tests and snapshots"
- goal: "see exactly what callers will experience via snapshot"
- frustration: "have to manually check each behavior for coverage"

**wisher:**
- pain: "features ship but break in ways i didn't expect"
- goal: "confidence that critical paths were tested"
- frustration: "test suite passed but experience is wrong"

---

## how much do they care?

| audience | priority | urgency | stakes |
|----------|----------|---------|--------|
| mechanic | high | immediate | affects their credibility and rework |
| foreman | high | review time | affects review quality and confidence |
| wisher | medium | ship time | affects outcome quality |

---

## what do they care about?

**mechanic:**
- success criteria: clear guidance, no surprises at verification
- tradeoffs: accepts more upfront work for less rework
- delight: self-reviews catch what they forgot

**foreman:**
- success criteria: can visually verify experience via snapshot
- tradeoffs: accepts more artifacts for better confidence
- delight: verification checklist references repros directly

**wisher:**
- success criteria: critical paths tested and frictionless
- tradeoffs: accepts slower route for better quality
- delight: sees the actual output in snapshot files

---

## how will they experience it?

**mechanic at repros:**
1. reads stone content with journey test sketch guidance
2. fills in table with BDD structure and input/output pairs
3. marks snapshot targets and critical paths
4. answers self-review prompts before the stone passes

**mechanic at verification:**
1. reads checklist that references repros artifact
2. fills in coverage table for each journey
3. confirms snapshots capture what was planned
4. answers self-review prompts before the stone passes

**foreman in review:**
1. opens snapshot files to see what caller would see
2. reads verification artifact to see coverage table
3. traces from wish → repros → verification → test file
